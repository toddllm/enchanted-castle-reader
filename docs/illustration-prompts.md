# Illustration Prompts - The Enchanted Castle (Full Book)

Prompting style follows the Nano-Banana Pro guide: use natural language, full sentences, clear subject/setting/lighting, and avoid tag soups. These prompts are intended for a consistent Edwardian storybook look.

## Art Direction (global)
- Edwardian storybook illustration with ink linework and soft watercolor washes.
- Subtle paper texture, gentle film-like grain.
- Clear focal point, readable silhouettes, cinematic framing.
- Warm daylight for day scenes, cool blue moonlight for night scenes.
- No modern objects, no anachronistic clothing.
- No text, captions, speech bubbles, watermarks, or signatures.
- 16:9 landscape, 2K to 4K output if available.

## Character Notes (global)
- Gerald: 12-13, slim, brown hair, confident; short trousers, collared shirt, vest.
- Jimmy: 11-12, shorter, stockier, sandy hair; short trousers.
- Kathleen: 12, slim, chestnut hair with ribbons or braids; pinafore dress.
- Mabel (Princess): 13-14, dark eyes, blue-black hair; simple dress or pink princess gown when enchanted.
- Mademoiselle: French governess, mid-20s, dark hair in a bun, refined.
- Lord Yalding: early 30s, country gentleman attire.
- Bailiff: tall, kind, grey flannel suit and straw hat on show-day.
- Phoebus: marble youth statue, classical features.
- Ugly-Wuglies: mannikins made from coats, hats, paper faces; one becomes a real man.
- Stone beast: massive dinosaur-like stone lizard.

## Character Consistency & References
- Use the reference images in `client/public/images/characters/` for Gerald, Jimmy, Kathleen, and Mademoiselle; match facial features, hair shape, freckles, and eye color.
- Mabel needs a reference image in the same style; keep her face consistent across all scenes once created.
- Keep chapter-specific wardrobes consistent across that chapter's scenes.
- All clothing must read as Edwardian (no jeans, T-shirts, sneakers, modern accessories).
- Avoid props not in the text (no swords, no modern items, no glowing effects unless explicitly described).

## Prompt Template (prepend or adapt as needed)
Base prompt: Create a storybook illustration for an Edwardian children's novel. Keep character faces consistent with the provided reference images. Use ink linework with soft watercolor washes, subtle paper texture, and a clear focal point. No text, no speech bubbles, no captions, no watermarks. No modern clothing or objects. 16:9 landscape.

## Naming Convention
Use `ch{chapter_num}_{scene_num}_{short_description}.png` (chapter and scene are 2 digits).

Example: `ch01_01_bandit_proposal.png`
